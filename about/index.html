About

Lambda Ladies is an online community for anyone who identifies as a girl or woman and is interested in functional programming. Complete beginners are welcome. The group has a Code of Conduct.

Lambda Ladies was formed by five functional programmers in March 2013. The main way members communicate is online, via a Google Group and Twitter. There is also a #lambdaladies channel on IRC's FreeNode network. In-person meetups and workshops are organised online.

Non-members who have a relevant functional programming job post or other announcement they would like posted to the Lambda Ladies mailing list can send it to the list moderators.

This website is open source. You are welcome to help us improve it.
